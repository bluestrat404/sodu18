South Durham Soccer Nights is an annual fun four-night soccer camp for kids 5-12, sponsored by South Durham Church.

Come see us one Sunday! We're here to glorify God by multiplying God-centered, big-hearted, hope-filled disciples of Jesus Christ. Worship and programs for children take place on Sundays, 4:00-5:15 p.m., at 4405 Hope Valley Rd., Durham, NC 27707.
